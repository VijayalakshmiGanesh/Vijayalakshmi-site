CHOLAS DYNASTY

Interesting Facts about Cholas other than regular school history book stories

The logo of cholas is a tiger but their strength lies in Water, because Cholas conquested more in water than land just like No one can defeat a crocodile in water. Cholas was the First Empire to have a Standing Army (professionally trained soldiers). Cholas had a strong and proper army 'RATHA GAJA THURADHI PADAI' which means Chariot, Elephants, Horse, and Cavalry.The places which were ruled by Cholas known as 'Chola Mandalam' later it was renamed as 'Coramandel Coast' by portuguese. Also, Roman coins were found in this places which indicates that they have done business with Rome. In China Records, it is registered that almost a thousand years ago, Cholas built an embassy in China for trade purpose which was a Profitable venture.

Their main port was located in Poompugar which is a town in mayiladuthurai district in Tamil nadu. In history, poompugar was a famous place just like how well known and significant place Chennai is now. This place 'Poompugar' is also described as an Architecturally marvellous city which had many international connections for import and export products which was built and developed by these Cholas people. They had a Tax system like Professional tax, Land Tax, Customs Tax which netizens felt difficult to pay it which at present is being followed with proper names to the taxes.

A Man- made lake, Currently named as 'Veeranam lake' was built by Cholas for their netizens which covered an area of length 11.2 kilometers and width 4 kilometers to avoid Water scarcity issue. Presently, it is one of the reservoirs which supplies water to Chennai city.

Coming to a Negative point, It is told that during the period of Cholas, the most caste related violence occured is with Shaivas (Lord Shiva followers) and Vaishanavas (Lord Vishnu followers). The reason behind this clashes is Cholas are mostly Shaivas . 108 temples were built across kaveri river area by a single chola king. thats how much cholas were devoting Lord shiva. So, while promoting the Shaivas religion, Vaishnavas devotees and Shaivas devotees had so much of fghts between them . There are also many theories which says King Raja raja chola did Sanskritization of Tamil (merged tamil and sanskrit). Thats why even now, we are able to see sanskrit words in temple walls and even Pandits are telling Sanskrit Mantras.

Chola captured Pandyas and ruled over many places successfully for almost 5 centuries and Pandyas came back with more power and took back their place from Cholas and put an end to Cholas dynastry.
