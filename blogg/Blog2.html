STORY OF PANDYAS

Who are Pandyas???

Pandyas was one of the ancient and powerful empires who majorly ruled South India and north of Sri Lanka. The name 'Pandya' came from "Pandaya" which means they are ancient people. When we hear the name Pandyas, the first thing that comes to mind is MADURAI MEENATCHI AMMAN TEMPLE. Here the word 'Meenatchi' can be splitted into "MEEN" which means Fish and "ATCHI" means Rule which depicts the logo/symbol of Pandyas (two fishes). And this Warrior princess 'Meenatchi' was ruling madurai and was married to Sundareshwarar , also refered as Lord Shiva and both of them were ruling the place. Here we can`t tell if its mythology or history. Thats how ancient pandyas are people.Even pandyas have been mentioned in Mahabharatha that they have fought alongside of Pandavas which proves the existence of empire. The above mentioned points can be a myth too. We are not sure.

Coming to history, When Marco Polo visited India, He has mentioned that so far, Pandyan dynasty is the Richest place . The reason is business. Their main port is tutucorin sea port and these people earned many resources by selling "Pearls", etc and also told that Pandyas are the people who took/harvested most of pearls and sold them. Also he has said they were so hygienic that each and every one of them had their own cups and plates. Also, they dont sip the tumblers while drinking. And, almost all of them were against consuming alcohols and the society weren't respecting people who were consuming it.

Pandyas liked the color 'Black'. From God Idols to important and familiar people, everyone were black. If a child was born with dark skin tone, their parents celebrated happily . This shows how much they liked and respected the color black. Pandyas associated the color 'White' with bad things and 'Black' with good fortunes which was quite different among other places. Last but not least, Pandyas had a tie up with Cheras, Pallavas and Sinhalese, and captured main places like Madurai, Uraiyur and Tanjore from Cholas almost completely.

Their business went so well with UAE which was noticed by a commander of Khilji's empire. As these Pandyas people were so wealthy and strong, Khilji's empire planned to capture pandyas. At the time, Pandyas were not strong since the princes were fighting among themselves for throne which gave the benefit to Khiljis and they captured Pandyas. Literally, they took all the resources from Pandyas. Almost a lakhs of Horses ( As Horse is not a native animal of India, they would have bought with money earned by doing business with Arabs for wars) and Thousands of Elephants. And that`s the wrap to Pandyan Dynasty..
